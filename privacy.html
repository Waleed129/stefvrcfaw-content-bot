Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information FBI VRP collects, how it is used, how it may be stored, and how users can request deletion or revoke access.

1. Service Overview

FBI VRP is a Discord-based publishing workflow that integrates with TikTok's official Content Posting API. The service allows authorized Discord users to connect an account, submit video files, captions, and hashtags for review, and publish approved content to a connected TikTok account.

The service includes account connection, request submission, moderation review, approval or rejection workflows, upload processing, publish status tracking, and related operational logging.

2. Information We Collect

To operate the service, we may collect and process the following categories of information:

  • TikTok authorization data: access tokens, refresh tokens, open ID, scope information, and token expiry metadata provided through TikTok OAuth.
  • Discord data: Discord user IDs, server IDs, channel IDs, role IDs, and interaction data required to process requests, approvals, permissions, and notifications.
  • Submission data: uploaded video files, file names, file size, captions, hashtags, request IDs, publish IDs, and request status data.
  • Moderation and audit data: approval actions, rejection actions, timestamps, status logs, and error messages necessary for workflow control, service reliability, and troubleshooting.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information only for legitimate service operations, including to:

  • authenticate and maintain the connected TikTok account integration;
  • receive, review, approve, reject, and publish submitted content;
  • show request status and moderation outcomes inside Discord;
  • enforce permissions, usage limits, and moderation controls;
  • maintain service security, reliability, and troubleshooting records.

We do not sell personal information, and we do not use collected information for advertising or marketing sales.

4. Storage and Retention

Some data may be stored locally or on infrastructure controlled by the service operator so the service can function correctly.

  • Uploaded videos may be stored temporarily while they are under review or being published.
  • TikTok authorization data may be stored so the service can continue authenticated publishing workflows.
  • Request records and logs may be stored for operational history, moderation review, and troubleshooting.

Data is retained only as long as reasonably necessary for service operation, security, troubleshooting, audit history, and compliance. Temporary uploaded files should be deleted after processing whenever reasonably possible.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent user data. Information may only be disclosed in limited cases:

  • to TikTok and Discord as necessary to perform the service through their official platforms;
  • when required by applicable law, regulation, legal process, or lawful authority;
  • to protect the security, integrity, and lawful operation of the service.

6. Third-Party Services

This service depends on third-party platforms. Your use of FBI VRP is also subject to the applicable policies of TikTok and Discord.

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • TikTok Terms of Service
  • Discord Privacy Policy

7. User Rights and Revocation

Users may revoke TikTok authorization through TikTok account settings at any time. Users may also request deletion of service-related submission records or operational data by contacting the project operator.

Support repository: github.com/Waleed129/stefvrcfaw-content-bot

8. Children's Privacy

This service is not intended for children under 13. Do not use the service if you are below the minimum age required by TikTok, Discord, or applicable law.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect information used by the service. However, no storage or transmission method is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
