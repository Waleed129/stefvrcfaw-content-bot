Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information when using the FBI VRP TikTok Bot.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the FBI VRP TikTok Bot ("we", "our", "the Bot") collects, uses, and protects information obtained through the use of our Discord bot integration with the TikTok API.

By using our Service, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • TikTok Account Data: Public profile information (username, display name, avatar) that you authorize via TikTok's OAuth login.
  • Discord User Data: Discord user IDs and server IDs necessary to deliver notifications.
  • Usage Data: Interaction logs with the Bot (commands used, timestamps) for service improvement purposes.
  • TikTok Content Metadata: Video titles, thumbnails, and engagement statistics for notification features — only from public TikTok accounts you connect.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the Bot's features and functionality
  • Send TikTok activity notifications to your Discord server
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Improve the Service based on usage patterns

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

Any data stored is kept secure using industry-standard practices. We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the Service. When you disconnect your TikTok account or remove the Bot, associated data is deleted within 30 days.

While we implement reasonable security measures, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to take precautions with your own account credentials.

5. TikTok API & Third-Party Services

This Bot integrates with TikTok's official API. Any data fetched from TikTok is governed by:

  • TikTok's Terms of Service
  • TikTok's Privacy Policy

We also operate within Discord's platform and are subject to Discord's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by applicable law or valid legal process
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users or the public
  • With your explicit consent

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Revoke TikTok API authorization at any time via TikTok's settings
  • Remove the Bot from your Discord server at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted to this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
